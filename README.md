# 🩺 Vitalyse AI

### *Decode Your Health. Improve Your Life.*

Vitalyse AI is an AI-powered personal health companion that helps users understand their medical reports, monitor their daily lifestyle habits, and receive personalized health insights—all in one intuitive platform.

## 🌟 Problem

Medical reports are often filled with complex terminology that can be difficult for patients to understand. At the same time, many people find it challenging to consistently track their daily habits and understand how these habits impact their long-term health.

## 💡 Solution

Vitalyse AI simplifies healthcare by transforming complex medical reports into easy-to-understand summaries while helping users build healthier lifestyles through AI-driven analysis of their daily activities.

## ✨ Features

* 📄 Upload blood tests, lab reports, prescriptions, and other medical documents.
* 🤖 AI-generated summaries in simple, easy-to-understand language.
* 📚 Instant explanations of complex medical terms and test results.
* ⚠️ Highlights abnormal values and important observations.
* 🍽️ Daily lifestyle tracking:

  * Food intake
  * Water consumption
  * Sleep duration
  * Physical activity
  * Stress levels
  * Mood tracking
* 📊 AI-generated Daily Health Score based on your lifestyle.
* 📈 Weekly and monthly health progress reports.
* 💡 Personalized recommendations to improve overall well-being.
* 🔔 Smart reminders for hydration, medications, exercise, sleep, and health check-ups.
* 📑 Compare previous and current medical reports to track health trends over time.
